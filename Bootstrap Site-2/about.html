Our Story

At PureLeaf Market, we believe tea is more than a beverage, it's a tradition, a culture, and a way of life. From the lush plantations of Sri Lanka to your cup, our mission is to deliver the highest quality Ceylon teas to tea lovers around the world. We are passionate about purity, flavor, and sustainability.

Founded in 2025, our team is committed to honoring the legacy of Sri Lankan tea growers by sourcing directly from ethical farms, using eco-friendly packaging, and supporting local communities.

Whether you're a lifelong enthusiast or new to the world of tea, PureLeaf Market welcomes you to explore authentic flavors that comfort, energize, and inspire.
